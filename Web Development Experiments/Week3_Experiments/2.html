Zoom in and Zoom Out using jQuery:

The purpose of this experiment was to use jQuery to zoom in the text and zoom out the text on page with button click.
Initially the font size is 1. Two functions zoomIn() and zoomOut() works on onclick event.
After zoom in if the text reaches boundary of window, the text realigns itself and further the font size increases.
