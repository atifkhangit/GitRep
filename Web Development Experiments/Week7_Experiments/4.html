Google news feed using RSS widget

rss feed widget 

Description:

In this experiment, the RSS feed of google news is read using a widget and displayed on this page. The RSS news feed format is in XML. This experiment is just to check how the rss feed can be displayed on the HTML page. A third party widget has been used to read the feed from XML and transform into readable content.
